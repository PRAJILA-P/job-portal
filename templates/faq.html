What is the name of your company

The name of our company is Jobzy. We are a leader in online recruitment and career services, dedicated to seamlessly connecting job seekers with their ideal roles and empowering companies to build their dream teams. Our flagship platform, Jobzy Talent Match, uses advanced AI and machine learning to match candidates to positions based not just on keywords, but on skills, culture fit, and career goals. With over 10 million job listings and a rapidly growing user base across North America and Europe, we are the trusted partner for professionals and employers worldwide.

How much pay for 3 months?

A three-month subscription to our Jobzy Pro Talent Search costs $599. This includes a 15% discount compared to the month-to-month rate, giving you full access to premium features like unlimited profile views, direct candidate messaging, and advanced filtering tools. Please visit our [Pricing Page](link to pricing page) for a detailed breakdown of all available plans.

Do I need to register?

Yes, you need to register to access the full range of our services. Job seekers must register to apply for jobs and save your preferred searches, while employers must register to post vacancies and access candidate profiles. Registration is quick, free for job seekers, and only requires a valid email address and a password.

Who should I contact in case of support.

For all support inquiries related to Jobzy, you have a few options: Email: Send an email to our dedicated support team at support@jobzy.com. We aim to respond within 24 hours. Live Chat: Visit our website and click the "Chat Now" icon in the bottom right corner for real-time assistance during our business hours (Monday-Friday, 9 AM - 5 PM EST). Help Center: Check out our comprehensive [Jobzy Help Center](link to help center) which contains articles, video tutorials, and guides for all platform features.
